# Reliance Jio Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-POL-003 · **Owner:** Corporate HR · **Last Reviewed:** 10 January 2026 · **Next Review Due:** 10 January 2027

## 1. Eligibility
- Hybrid working is available to employees in roles classified as **hybrid-eligible** by their department head.
- Field, retail, network-operations and customer-facing roles are office/site-based by default.

## 2. Hybrid Pattern
- Standard pattern: **3 days in office, 2 days remote** per week.
- Anchor days (all-team office days) are set by each department; Wednesday is the company-wide anchor day.
- Fully remote weeks require advance approval from the Line Manager and may not exceed 2 weeks per quarter.

## 3. Workspace & Security
- Remote work must be performed from within India.
- Company data may be accessed only on managed devices over the corporate VPN.
- Confidential HR data (salaries, ratings, personal records) must **never** be downloaded to personal devices or shared over personal email/messaging.

## 4. Performance & Availability
- Core collaboration hours: **10:00–16:00 IST**, regardless of location.
- Performance is assessed on outcomes, not visible online status.

## 5. Exceptions
- Medical or caregiving exceptions are handled case-by-case by the HR Business Partner with department head sign-off.
