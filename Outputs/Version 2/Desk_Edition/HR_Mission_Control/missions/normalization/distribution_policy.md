# Rating Distribution Policy (FY 2025-26)

Target distribution per business unit (tolerance plus/minus 5 percentage points):

| Rating | Target |
| :--- | :--- |
| Outstanding | 10% |
| Exceeds Expectations | 25% |
| Meets Expectations | 55% |
| Needs Improvement | 10% |

Calibration rules: distribution applies per BU per grade band where headcount permits; exceptions require HR Head sign-off with documented rationale; no employee rating may be changed without the manager being informed before release.
