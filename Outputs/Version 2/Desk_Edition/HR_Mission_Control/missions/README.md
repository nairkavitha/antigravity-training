# Monday Morning Library · Mission Data Packs

Ten folders, one per mission in the portal's 🗂 Missions tab. All data is **synthetic**; no real people, no real cases. Open the portal, pick a mission, and its brief tells the agent exactly which files here to read.

Standing rule: missions touching statute or employee rights (PIP, POSH, F&F, CTC) produce drafts for professional review. Your Legal and Compliance teams clear anything real.
