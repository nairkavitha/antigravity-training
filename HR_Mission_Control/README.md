# HR Mission Control - Local Sandbox

Welcome to the **HR Mission Control** local sandbox! This is your safe-to-fail environment where you will practice transitioning from manual tasks to **Agentic AI Orchestration** using **Antigravity 2.0**.

Here, you will not write code. Instead, you will work like a director or manager, delegating business goals to your "Digital Intern" (the Antigravity agent).

## Directory Structure

- 📁 `data/`
  - 📄 `employees.csv`: A mock employee database containing names, roles, grades, performance ratings, current salaries, and email addresses.
  - 📄 `market_rates.csv`: Market salary range data (minimum, midpoint, maximum) for Grades 1 through 4 in Indian Rupees (INR).
- 📁 `policies/`
  - 📄 `compensation_guidelines.md`: Corporate rules regarding overall budgets, rating-based salary increases, market adjustments, data privacy, and mandatory human sign-off checkpoints.
- 📁 `templates/`
  - 📄 `compensation_letter_template.md`: A template for the annual compensation adjustment letter to be generated for each employee.
- 📁 `exercises/` *(Created during the workshop)*
  - This folder will hold the logs, plans, and outputs you create together with the agent throughout the 4-day course.
- 📄 [glossary.html](../glossary.html): An Interactive AI Glossary of Agentic AI terms built specifically for HR professionals. Double-click this file to open it in your browser.
  - **3D Study Flashcards**: Flip cards to view non-technical corporate analogies and real-world Jio HR sandbox examples.
  - **Bulk Import**: Excel-compatible CSV bulk uploader with real-time preview, headers validation, and a downloadable CSV template.
  - **Restore Defaults**: Easily reset modifications and restore deleted terms back to defaults.
  - **Easy Editing**: Double-click any card to edit, or use the ✏️ and 🗑️ icons to modify/delete cards on the fly.


## How to Interact with the Sandbox
In each exercise, you will prompt the Antigravity agent (in the chat panel) by defining the business goal you want to achieve. 

### Core Agentic Workflow:
1. **Goal Request**: You describe the final outcome (e.g., *"Assess our current pay competitiveness against market benchmarks and flag any underpaid staff"*).
2. **Reviewing the Plan**: The agent will create an **Implementation Plan** (`implementation_plan.md`) describing the steps it plans to take. It will wait for your review.
3. **Approving the Plan**: Check its proposed steps, ensure it aligns with our policies, and type your approval or request revisions.
4. **Execution**: The agent will execute the task and track progress in `task.md`.
5. **Human Audit**: Once completed, review the output generated by the agent before taking any further action.
